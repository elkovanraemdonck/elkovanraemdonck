elko van raemdonck

Hello! I am Elko Van Raemdonck, a 17-year old visual art student who currently lives in Antwerp, Belgium. I study visual arts at the kunsthumaniora in Antwerp.

My main interests are photography, videography and graphic design. I am still learning a lot and would like to gain some more experience. I also like sports, I cycle regularly and I go to the gym 3 times a week.

I took most of my photos for school projects and sometimes also for free work. That is why I would now like to do some more outside-school projects to gain more experience.
